## Background

The Department for Business and Trade (DBT) promotes British trade and investment worldwide, supports UK businesses, and negotiates free trade agreements. With trade data spanning hundreds of markets and thousands of product categories, AI can transform how DBT identifies export opportunities, assesses market risks, and supports UK businesses in international trade.

## Project Scope

| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Trade Intelligence** | AI analysis of global trade flows, tariff impacts, and market opportunities |
| **Export Opportunity Matching** | ML-driven matching of UK businesses to export opportunities |
| **Market Risk Analytics** | Predictive models for trade disruption, sanctions impact, and geopolitical risk |
| **Investment Screening** | AI-assisted National Security and Investment Act screening |
| **Business Support Triage** | NLP-powered routing and prioritisation of business support requests |
| **Trade Agreement Analysis** | AI tools for modelling trade agreement impacts and compliance |
